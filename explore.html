Tuesday Market Secret

The weekly market near RTC Bus Stand (Tuesdays 6am-1pm) has authentic Lambadi tribe jewelry at half the price of tourist shops. Look for Lakshmamma's stall (blue umbrella) - she keeps the best pieces hidden under the counter for serious buyers. Bargain politely!
